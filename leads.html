Learn how to use this tool

Start with LinkedIn Sales Navigator and build a list of leads you'd like to target. Select the whole list and paste it into the text field at the top of this page.

If you can find someone's email address at that organization you can try to infer the "pattern" -- for example, droman@cloudera.com implies that people here usually use first initial followed by last name @cloudera.com.

This tool uses your leads' name to infer their email address based on the pattern you've provided. The whole output table can be copied and pasted into your own spreadsheet to track your email campaign. See the screenshots below for examples.
